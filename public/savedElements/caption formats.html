180 copies of this 1282-page Bible that has 42 lines per page, with two pages printed on each side of a leaf. The first print run sold out almost immediately, and around 50 complete copies survive today. This leaf, one of two in the Newberry’s collection, shows portions from two chapters of the book of Ezekiel.
